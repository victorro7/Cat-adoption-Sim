Pet Care

Important informations to help you take care of the little angels!

0-12 Weeks

Rapid Development Stage

During this period, the Kittens will develop the ability to see and hear as well as begin the process of walking. They begin grooming themselves as well as use a litter nearing 6-8 weeks. Additionally, teeth have developed.

Learn more

3-6 Months

Maturing Process

During this period, a kitten will spend less time actually sleeping. Teething occurs where the kittens chew on furniture and toys to help baby teeth fall out. By the end of this period, a majority of cats have become sexually mature.

Learn more

7-12 Months

Transitioning Into Adulthood.

Once this period begins, the decision to neuter or spay the cat should be made. The cat will also sleep more during this phase as the transition into adulthood is being made.Additionally, the cat should be transitioned to adult cat food during this period as well.

All About Cats

All the little angels you could possibly wish to adopt!

Pros

  • Petting a cat can have calming effects and reduce stress
  • Cats are entertaining
  • They may help control pests
  • They don't require much space
  • Cats don't need to be walked

Learn more

Cons

  • Cats shed fur
  • Cat owners need to clean the litter box regularly
  • They may claw the furniture
  • Veterinary bills can be very expensive
  • Cats may wake you up at odd times

Learn more

Considerations

  • Lifestyle - Do you have time to take care of a cat? Can you tolerate scratched up furniture?
  • Cat allergies
  • Kittens vs Adult Cats - Kittens need more attention and are more destructive. Adult cats have more stable personalities.
